Smart Protection
Your numbers and facts are always safe
Before compression starts, Promptolian locks every number, URL, file path, and technical name in your prompt. They are restored exactly after compression — never touched or changed. In tests across 100 real-world prompts, 98.4% of facts survived perfectly. When a prompt is very fact-heavy (like a legal contract), Promptolian automatically uses lighter compression so nothing important gets lost.

Get started

Install the extension

Add Promptolian to Chrome or Firefox from the extension store. No account required — it's free to start.

Step 2

Open any AI chat

Visit Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, or Perplexity. You'll see the Promptolian toolbar appear automatically in the text box.

Step 3

Type your prompt and compress

Write your prompt as normal, then click Compress. See how much shorter it got — then send. One click to restore the original any time.

The extension tracks how many tokens you've saved over time. Upgrade to Pro or Developer to unlock deeper compression and save even more.
