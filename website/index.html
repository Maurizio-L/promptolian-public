Smart Protection
Your numbers and facts are always safe
Before compression starts, Promptolian locks every number, URL, file path, and technical name in your prompt. They are restored exactly after compression — never touched or changed. In tests across 100 real-world prompts, 98.4% of facts survived perfectly. When a prompt is very fact-heavy (like a legal contract), Promptolian automatically uses lighter compression so nothing important gets lost.
