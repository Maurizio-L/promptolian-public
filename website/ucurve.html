The U-Curve: Why Over-Compression Costs More

Total tokens to task completion = context tokens + rework tokens. The minimum isn't at maximum compression.

Total (context + rework) Context tokens Rework tokens
Why the curve turns back up: At very high compression rates, the context engine or LLM summariser must discard specific facts — exact values, resource names, numbers. When the agent needs those facts later and can't find them, a human has to intervene. Each intervention is a rework event. At ~99% compression (Anthropic/OpenAI built-in), rework tokens dominate — often exceeding the original context cost.
